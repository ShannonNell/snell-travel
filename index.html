About Me

Aloha! Shannon is the name and travelling is the game. Unfortunately right now, the travel game is not afoot -dang COVID. But you can bet your bottom dollar that tomorrow, there'll be travel. And as soon as I can, I'll be there.

Ideal Adventures

Surfing:

Wherever there's water

Surfing is a passion of mine. Or it would be if I lived by the ocean. For now I'm just a wannabe surfer and if I ever get the chance, I take it.

Hiking:

Coastal mountains preferably

I used to hate hiking but have finally come to appreciate it. Having a camera and seeing the beauty of the world through a new 'lens' helped me appreciate it all the more. But maybe don't ask me to hike all day without access to some sort of waterfront.

Tours:

Boating, helicoptor rides, city walks

Tours have their ups and downs but I choose to focus on the good! Sometimes the tourguide is simply a great resource of information. Other times they take you to remote locations you wouldn't be able to access on your own. They teach you things, and if you can get a good group, it can be a blast!
